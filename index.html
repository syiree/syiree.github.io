Biography


John B. Benton, of The John Benton Law Firm, has been engaged in litigation of corporation and business transactions, specializing in complex and difficult cases where substantial interests are at risk, with principal offices in Houston, since 1984. Other significant areas include probate, personal injury, building construction issues, adverse possession of real property, and oil and gas drilling and related issues. He received a business and two law degrees from The University of Texas, and has been licensed to practice in Texas, New York, the federal district courts in Texas, the 5th Circuit Court of Appeals, and the United States Supreme Court.
